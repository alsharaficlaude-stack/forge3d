The full pipeline

One tool from idea to printer

Most projects bounce between a modeler, a repair tool and a slicer. Forge3D does the whole path in one place.

Everything in one place

Create your account to save your designs, pick up where you left off, and unlock the full toolset — design, edit, repair and export.

🧩

100+ editable shapes

Prisms, gears, stars, polyhedra, beams, lathed forms and more — each with live parameters (sides, teeth, holes) and exact mm sizing.

📥

Import & break STLs

Drop in any STL, split it into its separate bodies, or plane-cut a big model into printable pieces — with a live preview.

✂️

Cut, merge & hollow

Real boolean operations make openings, fuse parts and shell solids to save filament — all on your device.

🔗

Print connectors

Pegs & matching holes (round, square, dovetail…) at every cut so printed pieces line up and glue together.

📐

Measure & units

Always-on dimensions, a distance tool, height-off-plate, and mm / cm / inch — accurate to the millimetre.

🖨️

Slice & export STL

Layer preview, rough print stats and one-click binary STL for Cura, PrusaSlicer or Bambu.

Make anything

From brackets to keepsakes

Functional parts, repairs, fixtures, props — if it prints, you can build it here.

How it was built

Designed & built with Claude

Forge3D wasn't hand-coded line by line. It was built in plain-English conversation with Claude, Anthropic's AI — every feature described, generated, tested in a live browser, and refined until it worked.

Illustrative example — how features take shape with Claude.

A conversation, not a codebase

Each capability below started as a sentence. Claude wrote the Three.js engine integration, an in-browser CSG boolean engine, 100+ parametric shape generators, the slicer preview and the binary STL exporter — then verified each one by actually running it.

The 3D editor & STL export

Viewport, gizmos, primitives and binary STL that prints upright.

A real boolean engine

Embedded CSG for watertight cut / merge / hollow — no external dependency.

Draw, measure & units

Sketch on the plate with live dimensions, in mm / cm / inch.

Import & break STLs

Split bodies and plane-cut big models, previewing every cut and connector.

100+ parametric shapes

A whole catalogue generated and made editable, with a live preview.

This site & admin

Landing, accounts and an admin panel — built the same way.

What is Claude? ↗
Under the hood

Fast, private, and built to feel effortless

🔒 Private by design

All modelling, slicing and export happen on your device. Your designs are never uploaded — save and load are just local files you control.

⚡ Smooth & responsive

A modern WebGL engine with on-demand rendering — it stays fluid while you work and calms down the moment you pause.

🧰 Everything in one place

Design, edit, repair, break down and export — no jumping between separate apps to get from idea to printable model.
